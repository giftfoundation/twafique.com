Manufacturing

We possess total capability in manufacturing of MV / LV Distribution Panels. All the products are in accordance with International Electro-Technical Commission (IEC) Regulations standards and also as per the standards of the local electricity authority (Kharamaa) & other utility authorities.

We can manufacture MV & LV distribution system of about QAR 10-15 Million per annum. All our products are 100% screened and tested in every stage to ensure for supply the quality engineering products.

We have our own in-house engineering tools to Design, Manufacturing and Testing equipments to suit any customised specification. Highly experienced and well trained production team under the expert guidelines from our expertised engineering team to have quality and user friendly products. And We assure that we make the finished products meeting customer requirements and satisfaction.
