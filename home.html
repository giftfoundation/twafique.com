“Welcome to Twafique Trading & Contracting W.L.L.”

Owing to out constant quest to innovation and strict commitment to quality, we have gained a position of repute in the domain of Trading, Supplying and Manufacturing a technically advanced range of LV / MV Panels, Control Panels, PLC & SCADA Panels, MCC Panels with Variable Frequency Drives, Starter Panels, Distribution Boards, Power Factor Correction Panels, ATS & MTS Panels, VFD, Soft Starter, Battery Charger, Cable Trays & Cables.

Our Company has established in the year of 2006. we manufacture and source range encompasses LV / MV Panels & PLC / SCADA Panels.

In addition, we specialize in offering a comprehensive range of Human Machine Interface System, Programmable Logic Controllers, Control Panels and a broad range of Sensors like Inductive, Ultrasonic, Photo-Electrics and Magnetic.

All these products that we offer are specifically manufactured using optimum high grade raw material and employing advanced techniques as per industry defined standards and norms.

It is due to the efficient support of our professionals that we have been able to source and manufacture products that are at par excellence. Right from selection of appropriate vendors to procurement and till delivery these professionals keeps a strict vigil so that we can deliver best in industry range.

Moreover, these professionals make judicious use of all the installed machines and equipment so that we can manufacture new generation Control Panels that meet and exceed the expectations of the clients.

In addition to these, our professionals assist us in rendering highly effective and reliable Services as per the specifications of the clients.
