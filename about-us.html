Chairman

Twafique Trading and Contracting W.L.L, a Qatar based business enterprise started operations in the year of march 2006 under the chairmanship of “Jassim Mohammed A.M. Al Badi” In a short span of time the group had marked its strong presence in the Industrial and Building arena in Qatar Region.

Engineering Excellence

Twafique Trading & Contracting W.L.L., is the manufacturer and solution provider for Control Panel & Medium Voltage & Low Voltage Switchgears / Electrical Panel Boards & Control automation systems. Our Company has established with Excellent Engineering specialists and constantly upgraded its engineering strength by providing online training tools.We have successfully implemented Power & Control systems to fulfill the needs and solution for Projects such as Oil & Gas Industries/ Petroleum projects. Our areas of expertise includes Project Management, Design, Integration, Supply, Testing, Commissioning and Maintenance. We extend our best services & supports after sales. We use ABB & HAVELLS Components for Control Panels & MV & LV Switch Boards. We assure to provide optimal engineering solutions meeting the clients requirements. We are also building the long term business association / successful partnerships with our clients on mutual trust and respect. We have PLC / SCADA system solution for complete Industrial automation. We capable to undertake turnkey contracts as well as up gradation, Annual Maintenance Contract, In-house Training programs etc.. for industrial / Infrastructure automation covering small, medium and large-scale projects.
